Death Becomes Her

Director: Robert Zemeckis

When a novelist loses her man to a movie star and former frenemy, she winds up in a psychiatric hospital. Years later, she returns home to confront the now-married couple, looking radiant. Her ex-fiance's wife wants to know her secret, and discovers that she took the same potion which grants eternal life to the person who drinks it as she herself already has taken.


Review:

Death Becomes Her is a dark comedy that cleverly explores vanity and immortality.
